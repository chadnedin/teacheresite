October 2025 Developing Hands-On Tech Curriculum Designing introductory Arduino and IoT projects highlighted the importance of tactile learning. Breaking down complex components, like circuits and tilt sensors, into interactive steps proved essential for keeping students engaged and building their early confidence with hardware.

Teaching Python, cybersecurity, and robotics to youth reinforced my commitment to accessible education and challenged me to adapt my instructional strategies for non-traditional, diverse settings.

Integrating tools like Logisim to teach digital logic and Boolean algebra demonstrated the value of visual learning tools. This block heavily refined my approach to structured assessment and providing constructive feedback on technical assignments.
